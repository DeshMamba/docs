Workflows (also called the **Composer**) are Chattrvox's visual automation builder. You connect a series of triggers and actions on a canvas to create sequences that run automatically — sending SMS follow-ups, updating your CRM, initiating callbacks, applying tags, or branching on call outcomes. Each workflow you build is attached to one or more AI assistants and fires whenever the attached event occurs.

## What you can automate

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Post-call SMS follow-up" icon="message">

  </Card>

  <Card title="CRM updates" icon="database">

    Route contacts down different paths based on whether a call was booked, answered, went to voicemail, or matched a custom outcome.
  </Card>

## How workflows connect to AI assistants

A workflow on its own doesn't run until you link it to an assistant. When a call handled by that assistant ends, the platform fires any active workflows attached to it. You can attach the same workflow to multiple assistants, or build assistant-specific flows for more targeted automation.

<Note>
  Workflows using a **Campaign** trigger are linked to a campaign rather than a specific assistant. They fire when a contact passes through that campaign.
</Note>

## Workflow statuses

Every workflow has one of three statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Saved but not running. The workflow will not execute even if its trigger fires. Use this while you are building. |
| **Active** | Deployed and live. The workflow executes automatically when the trigger condition is met. |
| **Paused** | Temporarily suspended. The workflow is preserved but will not execute until you resume it. |

You can toggle between active and paused at any time from the workflow list or from inside the editor.
