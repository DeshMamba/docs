The GoHighLevel trigger fires when a new contact is created in your connected GoHighLevel account.

Use it to call new leads the moment they enter your GHL pipeline.

## Requirements

GoHighLevel must be connected under **Settings → Integrations** before this trigger is available.

## Configuration

No additional configuration required. The trigger watches for any new contact created in your connected GoHighLevel account.

**Available event:** Contact Created.

## Available variables

Contact details passed through from GoHighLevel are available as variables in downstream action nodes, including name, phone number, and email.

See [Variables](/workflows/variables) for the full variable reference.

## Use case

A contact fills out a GoHighLevel landing page form. The trigger fires immediately, a Wait node pauses for two minutes, then a Call Lead action dials the contact while the lead is still warm.
