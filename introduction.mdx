Chattrvox is an all-in-one AI voice platform built for agencies and sales teams that need to deploy conversational AI at scale. Instead of stitching together separate tools for calling, scheduling, CRM, and messaging, you get every piece of the pipeline in a single workspace — from the AI phone agent itself to the workflows that run after every call.

The platform is white-label ready, which means agencies can brand the entire experience under their own name and domain, then manage multiple clients through isolated workspaces without any of their data mixing together.

## Who Chattrvox is for

Chattrvox is designed for three types of users:

- **Agencies** that need to offer AI voice services to clients under their own brand, with separate workspaces and usage controls per client.
- **Sales teams** that want to run outbound calling campaigns, qualify leads automatically, and sync outcomes into their CRM without manual effort.
- **Business operators** that need inbound AI coverage — appointment scheduling, customer service, or lead capture — running around the clock.

## Key use cases

**Appointment setting** — Your AI agent calls prospects, qualifies interest, checks your calendar, and books meetings directly. A post-call workflow can confirm the booking by SMS and update your CRM in the same step.

**Outbound sales** — Launch a campaign against a contact list and let the AI handle the first conversation. Agents extract structured data from every call — name, company, sentiment, outcome — so your team only spends time on warm leads.

**Customer service** — Inbound callers are handled by an AI trained on your knowledge base. Common questions get answered immediately; complex issues are escalated with a full call summary.

**Lead follow-up** — Connect Chattrvox to Facebook Lead Ads or HubSpot so new leads trigger an immediate AI call, before they go cold.

## How workspaces work

Every resource in Chattrvox lives inside a workspace: your assistants, campaigns, contacts, and workflows. You can create multiple workspaces to separate teams, clients, or use cases. Agency accounts can manage client workspaces independently, each with its own minute allocation, members, and credentials.

<Note>
  If you are on a white-label plan, you can activate your own custom domain and branding from **Settings → Workspace → Whitelabel**.
</Note>
