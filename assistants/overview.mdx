AI assistants are the core of Chattrvox. Each assistant is a fully configurable AI voice agent that answers or places phone calls on your behalf, following the instructions you write, using the voice you choose, and capturing the data you care about after every conversation.

## What you can do with an assistant

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Inbound support" icon="headset">
    Handle incoming calls 24/7 — answer FAQs, collect caller information, and route to a human when needed.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Outbound sales" icon="phone-arrow-up-right">
    Dial contact lists automatically, deliver a consistent pitch, and log outcomes for every call.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Appointment booking" icon="calendar">
    Connect your Cal.com calendar and let the assistant book, confirm, and reschedule appointments in real time.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Lead qualification" icon="filter">
    Ask qualifying questions, score interest, and capture structured data like budget, timeline, and contact details.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom system prompt">
    Write the instructions your assistant follows on every call. Define its persona, conversation goals, what to say when, and how to handle edge cases — all in plain language.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Voice selection">
    Choose from a library of Cartesia voices across multiple languages and accents. Preview any voice before going live and adjust speaking speed to match your brand's tone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Structured data extraction">
    Configure fields — name, email, budget, appointment status, and more — that the AI automatically captures from the conversation transcript after every call.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Knowledge base">
    Attach a knowledge base of PDFs, web pages, or plain text. The assistant retrieves relevant information during calls to answer questions accurately without hallucinating.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Post-call SMS">
    Send an automatic SMS after every call using a customizable template. Useful for booking confirmations, follow-up links, or opt-in flows.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Call analysis">
    After each call, the AI generates a plain-English summary, scores the call against your definition of success, and writes structured data to your call history — ready for workflows or CRM sync.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Assistant lifecycle

Getting an assistant live takes four steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create">
    Name your assistant and open the configuration wizard. You can start from scratch or pass a name and workflow ID via URL to pre-fill fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Work through the three configuration tabs — **Model** (prompt, voice, language, integrations), **Analysis** (data capture, call scoring), and **Advanced** (call transfer, recording, web widget). Save at any point; your changes persist immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a phone number">
    Import a Twilio number under **Agents → Phone Numbers**, then assign it to your assistant on the Advanced tab. The assistant will answer every inbound call to that number from the moment you save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Make a test call to verify the greeting, voice, and instructions work as expected. Once confirmed, your assistant is live and handling real calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need a Twilio account and at least one imported phone number to receive inbound calls. See **Agents → Phone Numbers** to add numbers, or connect your Twilio credentials under **Settings → Integrations**.
</Note>
