## How Chattrvox works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an AI Assistant">
    Configure your agent's prompt, voice, and knowledge base. Connect a phone number to make it live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Campaigns or Go Inbound">
    Upload your contact list and launch an outbound dialing campaign, or route inbound calls to your assistant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automate Follow-ups">
    Use the visual Workflow Builder to send SMS, update your CRM, or trigger calls automatically after conversations end.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Optimize">
    Analyze call transcripts, summaries, outcomes, and sentiment from your dashboard to continuously improve results.
  </Step>
</Steps>
